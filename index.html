About Me

Engineer, builder, and curious mind from Haiti 🇭🇹

Hello! I'm SENATUS Wood Velsen, a Haitian electrical engineering student currently pursuing my degree at Yuan Ze University (YZU) in Taiwan. My fascination with how electricity shapes our modern world drives me to design, prototype, and innovate at the intersection of hardware and software.

From designing analog circuits to programming microcontrollers, I love tackling complex problems. My recent focus has been on renewable energy systems and IoT-enabled embedded devices — technologies I believe will shape a more sustainable future.

Beyond the lab, I contribute to open-source hardware communities, explore Taiwan's vibrant culture, and constantly tinker with side projects that push my skills further.
